- TITLE (TAJUK): the article's title

- All metadata: Author, Date, Affiliation, Publisher, ISSN, DOI, Email, Phone, Address

- Appendices, glossaries, table of contents
- Short figure/table labels without context

CLEAN_TEXT:
TITLE:
